## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Key Business Risks

1. **Market Competition**
   - Risk: Large incumbents with significant resources
   - Mitigation: Focus on underserved SME segment, superior UX
   - Probability: Medium | Impact: High

2. **Customer Acquisition Cost**
   - Risk: Higher than expected CAC affecting profitability
   - Mitigation: Product-led growth, viral loops, partnerships
   - Probability: Medium | Impact: Medium

3. **Technology Scalability**
   - Risk: Platform unable to handle growth
   - Mitigation: Microservices architecture, cloud infrastructure
   - Probability: Low | Impact: High

4. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - Risk: Data privacy and security regulations
   - Mitigation: Proactive compliance, regular audits
   - Probability: Medium | Impact: Medium

5. **Economic Downturn**
   - Risk: Reduced business spending on fleet management
   - Mitigation: Value proposition focused on cost savings
   - Probability: Low | Impact: High

### Financial Risks

1. **Revenue Recognition**
   - Risk: Complex revenue recognition affecting financials
   - Mitigation: Clear subscription model, ASC 606 compliance
   - Probability: Low | Impact: Medium

2. **Cash Flow Management**
   - Risk: Running out of cash before profitability
   - Mitigation: Conservative burn rate, milestone-based funding
   - Probability: Medium | Impact: High

3. **Currency Risk**
   - Risk: International expansion currency fluctuations
   - Mitigation: Natural hedging, forward contracts
   - Probability: Medium | Impact: Low
